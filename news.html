Instructions



Please make sure your records are fully updated on the NASIMS portal before taking this test

For first time login, recover your password using the underlisted procedures:

1. Click on Login
2. Use the "Forgot Password" Option
3. Select either N-POWER application email or N-POWER application ID and newly created password
4. Enter the information of the option selected
5. Click on "Reset Password" 6. Enteryour new password and click proceed 7. Login with your N-POWER appljcation email or N-POWER application ID and newly created Password

After successful reset of passowrd, please login to updateyour records before taking test.

Note

This test is time base and you are expected to answer 20 questions in 10 mins.

1. Test cannot be retaken after submission 2. You cannot skip any question 3. Every question is allotted equal score 4. If you are timed out after the third attempt without submitting, you will not be able to take the test again.
